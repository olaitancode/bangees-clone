Our Story

Our journey started with a simple dream—to create a space where people could come together, relax, and enjoy life’s little moments. Ogbomosho is a city full of vibrant energy and culture, but we felt there was something missing: a placewhere students, indigenes and visitors could unwind, connect, and be inspired. That’s where the idea for Bangees Café was born.


What makes Bangees Café special? It’s the little details. From the warm lighting to the comfy seating, we’ve carefully crafted every part of the café to offer you a relaxing and beautiful space. Our interiors are a mix of modern and classic, with splashes of color and art. It’s the kind of place where you can snap a few great photos, but more importantly, feel at ease and truly yourself


Beyond food and drinks, Bangees Café is about community. Ogbomosho has a special place in our hearts, and we wanted to build something that brings people together. Whether you’re a student working on a project, a professional taking a break, or someone just looking to relax, Bangees Café is your space.

Our Menu

At Bangees Café, we believe that food and drinks should be as comforting as they are exciting. That’s why we’ve crafted a menu that combines classic favorites with unique twists—so whether you’re here for your quick lunch, or a late-night treat, we’ve got something that’s just right for you.


We’re passionate about using fresh, quality ingredients in everything we serve, and you’ll taste that passion in every bite and sip. Our menu is designed to satisfy your cravings, whatever the hour.
